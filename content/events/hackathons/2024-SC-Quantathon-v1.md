## About the Event

The SC Quantathon v1 was the inaugural quantum computing hackathon organized by SC Quantum, held at the Darla Moore School of Business at the University of South Carolina. The event featured challenge tracks from IonQ, Xanadu, and DoraHacks, with the grand prize being an all-expenses-paid trip to the NYUAD International Hackathon.

The event took place October 10–13, 2024 in Columbia, SC.
