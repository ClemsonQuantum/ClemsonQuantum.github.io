## About the Event

The NYUAD International Hackathon for Social Good is an annual event focused on innovation in computer science and engineering, emphasizing quantum computing and AI. Students from around the globe come together for a weekend of building, learning, and competing.

The event took place April 25–27, 2025 at New York University Abu Dhabi.
