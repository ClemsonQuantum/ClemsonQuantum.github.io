## About the Event

The SC Quantathon v2 was the second annual quantum computing hackathon organized by SC Quantum, held at the Darla Moore School of Business at the University of South Carolina. Building on the success of v1, the event brought together teams from across the Southeast.

The event took place October 9–12, 2025 in Columbia, SC.
