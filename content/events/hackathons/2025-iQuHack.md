## About the Event

iQuHACK (interdisciplinary Quantum HACKathon) is MIT's annual quantum computing hackathon and one of the largest events of its kind. Organized by MIT's iQuISE student group, it brings together students from around the world to build quantum projects using real quantum hardware.

The event took place January 31 – February 2, 2025 at the Stata Center, MIT. It featured both in-person and remote tracks with challenges from industry sponsors.
